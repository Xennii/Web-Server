Major Factions of the Galaxy

The galaxy is divided among countless powers, each fighting for dominance in an endless war.

THE IMPERIUM OF MAN

Colors: Blue and Gold | Founded: M25 | Population: Quadrillions

The largest human empire in the galaxy. Led from the Holy World of Terra by the immortal God-Emperor, the Imperium stretches across thousands of inhabited worlds. Despite its vastness, it stagnates under bureaucratic weight and superstition, slowly losing worlds to enemies and decay.

Strength: Vast numbers, ancient technology, Space Marines, faith

Weakness: Stagnant, corrupt, losing territory

FORCES OF CHAOS

Colors: Black and Red | Founded: Prehistoric | Powers: Dark Gods

Servants of the Chaos Godsâ€”Khorne the Blood God, Nurgle the Plague Lord, Tzeentch the Changer, and Slaanesh the Dark Prince. These corrupted warriors and daemons seek to plunge the galaxy into eternal darkness. They are powered by human sin, emotion, and bloodlust.

Strength: Daemonic powers, endless numbers, corruption

Weakness: Infighting, unpredictable, no unified command

THE AELDARI

Colors: Purple and Blue | Founded: Ancient past | Status: Endangered

An ancient, proud race of psychic warriors who once ruled the galaxy. Now they hide in their craftworlds, fleeing the predations of Slaanesh, who hunts their souls. Some have embraced darkness as Dark Eldar, becoming raiders and torturers.

Strength: Psychic powers, ancient technology, speed

Weakness: Few in number, hunted, arrogant

ORKS

Colors: Green and Yellow | Founded: Engineered by Old Ones | Nature: Brutal

Brutish, warlike creatures who exist solely for conquest and battle. Their crude technology works through belief and luck. They spread like locusts, their WAAAGHs consuming entire sectors. Yet they are cunning in their own savage way.

Strength: Incredible ferocity, numbers, regeneration

Weakness: Unorganized, lack advanced tech, easily distracted

NECRONS

Colors: Silver and Black | Founded: M1 (ancient) | Status: Awakening

Ancient synthetic beings who ruled the galaxy billions of years ago. They lay dormant in their tombs until recently. Now they awaken, seeking to reclaim their dominion and eradicate all organic life. Their technology is incomprehensible to modern minds.

Strength: Ancient tech, immortality, numbers, no emotion

Weakness: Fragmented dynasties, awakening incomplete

THE TAU EMPIRE

Colors: White and Red | Founded: M35 | Philosophy: The Greater Good

A young but rapidly expanding empire of psychically-null aliens. They seek conquest through diplomacy and collaboration under their philosophy of "The Greater Good." Many suspect hidden puppet masters control their expansion.

Strength: Advanced technology, philosophy, gunlines

Weakness: Young, small population, weak in melee
