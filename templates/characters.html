The Legends of Warhammer 40K

Heroes and villains whose deeds shaped the destiny of countless worlds.

The God-Emperor of Mankind

Status: Immortal | Age: Unknown (over 10,000 years)

The most powerful psychic being in existence. He guides the Imperium through the Astronomican and watches all of humanity through countless visions. His body is corpse-like, sustained only by technology and human sacrifice. Yet his will is absolute.

Power: Unmatched psychic ability, immortality, galaxy-spanning presence

Roboute Guilliman

Status: Alive | Age: 10,000+ years (suspended animation)

One of the Emperor's greatest sons, now awakened after 10,000 years of sleep. His tactical genius and administrative acumen offer the Imperium new hope. Yet he struggles to understand the dogma-bound Imperium his father's institution has become.

Skills: Tactical genius, leadership, immortal warrior, diplomacy

Abaddon the Despoiler

Status: Alive | Allegiance: Chaos Powers

One of the Emperor's sons, now a traitor and the leader of the Black Legion. For 10,000 years, he has waged war on the Imperium with his army of the damned. After 13 failed Black Crusades, his 14th has succeeded in destroying Cadia itself.

Power: Daemonic strength, cursed armor, commanding presence, immortality

Corvus Corax

Status: Corrupted | Allegiance: Lost to Chaos

The Raven Guard Primarch, lost to madness and the Warp itself. Once a master of shadows and guerrilla tactics, Corvus Corax has become a tormented daemon-prince between man and something far worse. Some whisper he still hunts the traitors from within the immaterium.

Powers: Shadow manipulation, psychic ability, daemon form, void travel | Last Sighting: Eye of Terror

Powers: Superhuman strength, rapid regeneration, leadership of billions | Belief: Gork & Mork incarnate

Szarekhan Dynasty

Status: Awakening | Era: 60+ Million Years | Philosophy: Extinction

The apex of Necron nobility, the Szarekhan Dynasty represents the pinnacle of a race older than stars themselves. Under the command of their renewed Silent King, these ancient mechanical beings view all organic life as temporary corruption to be purged. Their technology transcends mortal comprehension.

Powers: Ancient technology, immortal warriors, FTL capabilities | Goal: Galactic supremacy

The Omnissiah

Status: Unknown | Form: Digital/Mechanical | Followers: Billions of Tech-Priests

The divine machine-god worshipped by the Adeptus Mechanicus. Whether ancient AI, daemon entity, or manifestation of the collective will of Mars itself remains unknowable. The Omnissiah's doctrines drive humanity's technological innovations and guide the preservation of archaic knowledge.

Dogma: The machine has a soul | Knowledge is power | Influence: Galaxy-wide | Mystery Level: Absolute
