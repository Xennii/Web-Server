The Imperium of Mankind

A sprawling galactic empire spanning thousands of worlds, held together by faith, fear, and the immortal will of the God-Emperor.

The Golden Throne

For ten thousand years, the God-Emperor has sat upon the Golden Throne of Terra, his consciousness reaching across the galaxy through the Astronomican—a psychic beacon that guides human vessels through the warp. An immortal consciousness sustaining an empire of quadrillions.

The Adeptus Organizations

The Imperium is divided into many militant and governmental institutions: the Space Marines—genetically enhanced super-soldiers who serve as the Imperium's finest warriors; the Imperial Guard—countless billions of infantry; and the Adeptus Mechanicus—machine priests who guard forbidden technology from a lost golden age.

Ten Thousand Worlds

From Paradise Worlds with perfect climates to Hive Worlds stacked with billions of souls, the Imperium rules through an ancient bureaucracy unchanged for millennia. Each world faces threats from within and without: xenos horrors, daemonic incursions, and the slow degradation of technology none remember how to build.

The Warp

Faster-than-light travel requires passage through the Warp—a hellish dimension where daemons hunt and time itself bends to breaking point. Many ships are lost to its horrors, emerging years in the future or the past. Navigation requires psychic guides and the grace of the God-Emperor himself.
